Hitting zero is useful. Knowing how far past zero you went is even more useful. Super Easy Timer can count up after the countdown ends.

## See your overtime

Turn on count up and the timer keeps going after it reaches zero. Instead of stopping, it shows how long ago the time ran out.

## Tighten your talk

Run your talk against the timer and watch the count up. It shows exactly where you ran long, so you can cut the right parts before the real thing.

## Keep meetings honest

In a meeting, count up makes overtime visible to everyone. The number keeps climbing until you wrap up.
