You set a timer, then realize you need a little longer. Most apps make you stop and start over. Super Easy Timer lets you adjust on the fly.

## Add time as you go

Type a change and keep the same countdown running:

- "Add 5 min"
- "+5m"

The time gets added without a reset.

## Remove time too

Going faster than planned? Take time off the same way:

- "Remove 1 hour"
- "-1h"

## Keep your place

The timer keeps running while you adjust. No restart, no reset, no losing track of where you were.
