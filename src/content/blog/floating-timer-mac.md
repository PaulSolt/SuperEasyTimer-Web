A timer only helps if you can see it. Super Easy Timer floats above your work, so the countdown stays in view while you do everything else.

## Float it above your windows

Turn on the floating mode and the timer sits above your other windows. It stays put while you type, click, and switch apps. Drag it wherever it fits best.

## Keep it small or make it big

Shrink the timer into a small floating window in the corner. Make it large when you want to read it from across the room. Either way, it stays on top.

## Never lose it

Edge guides and snap-back keep the floating timer from drifting off screen. It stays where you can find it, every time.
