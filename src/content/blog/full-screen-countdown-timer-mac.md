Sometimes the whole room needs to see the time. Super Easy Timer turns your Mac into a full screen countdown with big, clear digits.

## Big digits, no clutter

Switch to full screen and the timer fills the display. The digits scale to fit, so they read clearly from the back of the room. Nothing else gets in the way.

## Built for shared screens

Use the full screen countdown for classes, talks, meetings, webinars, and any shared display. Set a time, start it, and let everyone watch the same clock.

## Pick the look that fits the room

Choose a theme that suits the space. A bright screen for a classroom, or a calm dark one for a talk. The timer keeps your theme in full screen.
