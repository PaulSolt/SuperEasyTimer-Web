A timer should fit the screen it sits on. Super Easy Timer gives you themes for any background, from bold color to barely there.

## Pick a theme

Choose the look that fits:

- Transparent and frosted to blend into your work
- Liquid Glass for a clean, modern feel
- Blue, red, black, white, and cream for bold, readable color

## Blend in or stand out

Use a transparent or frosted theme to keep the timer subtle while you work. Switch to a bold color when you want it to read from across the room.

## Stay out of the way

Controls and the text input fade away when you are not editing the timer. You see the time, not the buttons. They come back the moment you need them.
