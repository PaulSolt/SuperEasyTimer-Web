The right size depends on the moment. Super Easy Timer resizes from a tiny corner timer to a full screen countdown.

## Make it big

Drag the timer larger when the whole room needs to read it. The digits stay clear and sharp at any size.

## Shrink it with Mini Mode

Pull it small and keep it in the corner while you work. Mini Mode gives you a tiny, glanceable timer that does not crowd your screen.

## Never lose it

Edge guides and snap-back keep the timer on screen, even when it is small. Drag it near an edge and it settles into place instead of slipping away.
