Most timers make you spin a dial or tap a keypad. Super Easy Timer lets you type what you mean and press Return.

## Type a duration

Type the time the way you would say it:

- "5 min"
- "20 minutes"
- "2h 30m"
- "25" means 25 minutes

A bare number is read as minutes, so "25" starts a 25 minute timer.

## Count down to a clock time

Add am or pm to count down to a time of day:

- "5pm"
- "2:15am"

The am or pm tells the timer you mean a clock time, not a duration.

## One field, no setup

There are no screens to dig through. Type, press Return, and the timer starts.
